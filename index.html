Guy Tevet

I'm a Ph.D. student in the Computer Graphics lab at Tel-Aviv University, advised by Dr. Amit Bermano and Prof. Daniel Cohen-Or. My main field of study is human motion generation. In the summer of 2023, I was a visiting research student at the University of British Colombia advised by Prof. Michiel Van de Panne and Dr. Xue Bin (Jason) Peng (Simon Fraser University).

Before that, I obtained my master's degree at Tel-Aviv University in 2020, researching the evaluation of natural language generation, advised by Prof. Jonathan Berant. I was a research intern at Google during 2020-2021 in natural language processing, and a computer vision researcher at Apple during 2018-2020.
