Guy Tevet


I am a Postdoctoral Scholar at Stanford University, where I take part in the Digital Athlete Moonshot team under the supervision of Prof. C. Karen Liu and Prof. Scott L. Delp.

I completed my Ph.D. in the Computer Graphics lab at Tel-Aviv University, advised by Prof. Amit Bermano and Prof. Daniel Cohen-Or. My research focuses on human motion generation. In the summer of 2023, I was a visiting research student at the University of British Columbia advised by Prof. Michiel Van de Panne and Prof. Xue Bin (Jason) Peng (Simon Fraser University).

Before that, I obtained my master's degree at Tel-Aviv University in 2020, researching the evaluation of natural language generation, advised by Prof. Jonathan Berant. I was a research intern at Google during 2020-2021 in natural language processing, and a computer vision researcher at Apple during 2018-2020.
